Aikido Training Gear

Aikido requires the minimum of training gear and equipment. Beginners can start their first class in a track suit or pair of loose fitting slacks and later, before their first grading, advance to a proper keiko-gi. (A heavy quality white Judo suit is preferable.)

Keiko-gi / kimono, hakama, books, and weapons such as bokken, jo and tanto can be ordered through the dojo. Please consult your instructor.
